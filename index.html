About BroadcastAI
Built with purpose. Designed for Indian marketers.
BroadcastAI  v2.0 — for Edu Tantr

A standalone, browser-based marketing automation tool that rewrites one message into platform-perfect copies for 8 social media channels — in 7 Indian languages, with 7 tone options.

Powered by: Google Gemini 2.0 Flash (Free API — ₹0 cost)
Platforms: WhatsApp · Instagram · Facebook · LinkedIn · YouTube · X · Telegram · Threads
Languages: English · Hindi · Tamil · Telugu · Kannada · Hinglish · Tanglish
Features: Campaign naming · History (30 sessions) · Export to text · Platform tips · Dashboard
Privacy: 100% local — no server, no tracking, no data leaves your device
Cost: ₹0 forever

Built as a contribution to Edu Tantr — Bridging Gaps, Building Bridges! 🎓
Designed to help the Edu Tantr marketing team broadcast course launches, offers, and updates across all platforms in seconds.
